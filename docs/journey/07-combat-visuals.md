# Dev Log: Combat Visuals (God Manifestations)

## Overview
The "Divine Support" mechanic was made more impactful by adding dramatic visual manifestations of the gods during Arena combat.

## Key Changes

### ✨ Divine Manifestations
- **God Summoning**: When a Divine Skill is used, the respective god (Leo, Arena, or Draco) now physically appears behind Kane.
- **Dramatic Entrance**: Added a sequence of visual "Summoning" effects:
    - **Magic Circle**: A glowing base appears on the floor.
    - **Screen Flash**: A full-screen color-coded flash based on the god's theme.
    - **Camera Shake**: A brief screen shake to convey power.
- **Floating Aura**: The god sprite has a "yoyo" floating animation to make them feel celestial and non-human.

### 🎨 Visual Polish
- **Color Coding**: 
    - **Leo**: Red (War)
    - **Arena**: White/Gold (Queen)
    - **Draco**: Green (Dragon/Nature)
- **Floating Text**: Improved the font and flair of the skill name display.

## Rationale
Gods are the central theme of the game. Previously, they only existed as text or portraits. By making them physically appear in the heat of battle, we emphasize their role as protectors and reinforce the "Bond" mechanic visually.
